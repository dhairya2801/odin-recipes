Chana Masala

This is a traditional vegetarian Indian dish mainly found in North regions of the country. This dish is highly rich in nutrients and mostly prepared for dinner. It is mainly served with Lassi as the side. Lassi is a drink which is made from curd and its taste is the perfect quantity of sweetness and thickness. I can confirm that after this heavenly meal, you are sure to have one of the best sleeps of your life. Check the recipe below to make it close to perfection!

Ingredients

  • Boiled chickpeas (save the broth)
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Kulcha (a type of Indian bread)
  • Green chillies
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Butter
  • Cumin seeds
  • Masala
    • Red chilli powder
    • Turmeric powder
    • Garam masala
    • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop the onions finely
  2. Prepare a gravy by blending tomatoes and green chilli
  3. Place 2tbsp butter in a pan on medium flame
  4. Add cumin seeds and 1tbsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Add the onions, wait till they shrink and get a golden brown color
  6. Add all masalas
  7. Add the gravy
  8. Add boiled chickpeas with 200ml broth, and let it cook for 10 mins
  9. Serve with lassi and Kulcha
  10. Enjoy
